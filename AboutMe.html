About Me

Hi. My name is Andy Mauvais and this is what you should know about me.

Currently, I am in my first year enrolled in Florida Gulf Coast Univeristy pursuing a major in Computer Information Systems with a minor in Statistics. One of my goals is to become a data scientist or be in a similar occupation.
I was born and raised in Fort Myers, Florida and am of Haitian descent. My passion for technology was developed back in high school (Dunbar High School). There, I was enrolled in many different technology classes that really helped build a foundation for who I am today.
Most people that meet me can agree that I am a very mellow and easy-going person, so it is easy for me to get along well with others.
